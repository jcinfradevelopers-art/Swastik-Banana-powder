About Swastik Banana

Swastik Banana is a trusted manufacturer and supplier in the agro-processing sector, committed to delivering high-quality, natural, and nutritious food ingredients. Based in Telangana, India, we specialize in Dehydrated Banana Powder and Organic Banana Powder.

We source premium-grade bananas directly from trusted farmers and use advanced dehydration and processing techniques to preserve natural taste, color, and nutritional value.

At Swastik Banana, we emphasize sustainability, hygiene, innovation, and customer satisfaction while supporting local farmers and promoting healthy food solutions.
